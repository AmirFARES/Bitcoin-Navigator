# Bitcoin Navigator: A Dashboard for Smarter BTC Investments 🚀💰

![Project Image](https://github.com/AmirFARES/Bitcoin-Navigator/blob/main/imgs/cover.png)

## Introduction 🌟

Welcome to **Bitcoin Navigator**, an interactive dashboard designed in Looker Studio to empower Bitcoin (BTC) investors. This tool provides insights into price trends for 2024 and early 2025, helping investors optimize their dollar-cost averaging strategy. While this is not financial advice, the dashboard offers data-driven insights to make informed decisions about when to invest in Bitcoin, both on macro and micro levels.

**Disclaimer**: This is not financial advice. The insights are based on personal research and preferences in the crypto market.

**[Bitcoin Nvigator Dashboard](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/e1d9de8a-5c36-4581-8c8b-1060893902f9)**

## About this Project 🌐

Bitcoin Navigator is tailored for crypto enthusiasts looking to understand market trends and optimize their investment strategies. By analyzing data from 2024 and early 2025, this dashboard identifies optimal times to invest in Bitcoin, ensuring investors maximize their returns by buying at favorable price points.

### Key Objectives 🎯

- **Visualize Price Trends**: Identify and track Bitcoin price trends across weeks and months.
- **Optimize Investments**: Provide actionable insights to enhance the dollar-cost averaging strategy.
- **Macro & Micro Analysis**: Explore trends across the entire year and specific periods (Nov 2024 – Jan 2025).
- **User-Centric Design**: Build a dashboard that’s easy to use and focused on investor needs.
- **Transparency**: Base insights on reliable data from Investing.com.

## Data Sources 📊

To ensure data accuracy, the dashboard relies on Bitcoin price data from the trusted platform [Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/). Key datasets include:

- **[Historical Daily Data (2024 – 2025)](https://github.com/AmirFARES/Bitcoin-Navigator/blob/main/data/Bitcoin%20Historical%20Data%202024.csv)**: Offers a comprehensive view of Bitcoin’s market behavior.

## Methodology 🚀

The dashboard was created using Looker Studio and incorporates data from 2024 and early 2025. Insights are driven by personal research and observations in the crypto market, with the goal of simplifying investment decisions for BTC holders.

### Key Findings 📈

1. **Weekly Trends**:  
   - **Macro Level**: Throughout 2024, **Tuesdays** and **Saturdays** consistently offer the best price points for investment.  
   - **Micro Level**: Between November 2024 and mid-January 2025, **Saturdays** remain the most favorable day for investing.

2. **Monthly Trends**:  
   - **Macro Level**: The **early days of the month** and the **week after mid-month** present the best opportunities to invest.  
   - **Micro Level**: The **first days of each month** are particularly favorable.

## Conclusion 🌱

While this dashboard is not financial advice, the data suggests that the optimal strategy for dollar-cost averaging is to invest on the **first Saturday of each month**. This approach balances both weekly and monthly trends, maximizing the potential for better returns.
